About RailBook

RailBook is an official initiative by Indian Railways to provide aspirants with a centralized platform for accessing Railway examination resources. This platform offers previous year question papers, practice tests, and study materials for various Railway recruitment examinations including RRB NTPC, RRB Group D, RPF Constable, and other technical and non-technical posts.

What makes RailBook unique is its ability to transform into a Progressive Web Application (PWA). This means you can install RailBook as an application on your mobile phone, tablet, or desktop computer. Once installed, you can access all resources offline, receive notifications about new content, and enjoy a seamless app-like experience across all your devices.
