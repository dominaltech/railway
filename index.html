About This Platform

The Indian Railways Question Bank is an official digital resource designed to assist candidates preparing for various railway recruitment examinations. This comprehensive platform consolidates question papers from multiple examination categories, providing aspirants with authentic study materials curated directly by Indian Railways authorities.

Our platform transforms seamlessly into a Progressive Web Application (PWA), enabling you to install it as a native application on your smartphone, tablet, or desktop computer. This conversion ensures offline accessibility, faster loading times, and an app-like experience without consuming significant device storage. The installation process is simple and works across Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems.

The question bank interface is optimized for all screen sizes, from compact mobile devices with 380px width to large desktop monitors. We have implemented responsive design principles ensuring consistent readability and usability regardless of your device. Navigate through categorized sections, download PDF materials, and track your preparation progress with an intuitive user interface designed specifically for Indian Railway examination candidates.
